The Schedule

The Marey Chart is a position ✕ time visualization that plots each vehicle on each line as it reaches each station. This widget is a great way to visualize train performance and, especially, makes it easy to see when delays occur. In particular, on the Green Line, you can sometimes observe ‘clumps’ of trains where branches become bottlenecked due to other vehicles within close proximity to them.

Station Timetables

The timetables are perhaps the most useful widget for everyday users. They provide a view of when the next five trains (either inbound or outbound) will reach a certain station. This is very similar to the status boards that can be found at most MBTA subway stations which display the next 2-4 incoming trains.
